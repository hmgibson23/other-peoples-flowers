Other People's Flowers

A podcast for rejected stories, essay, and poems.

About

Other People's Flowers is a podcast to give voice to stories, essays, or poems that have been rejected elsewhere.

We give these rejected pieces voice by reading them out on our podcast. We don't mind where you're from, who you are, what you do, and what your political inclincations might be. We just want stories that got rejected.

If you'd like to have your piece showcased, please see our submission guidelines below

Submit

Please send your rejected pieces to editor@otherpeoplesflowers.org. Please include the title of the work and your name in the email title.

Pieces should be no longer than 6,000 words.

Manuscripts should be double-spaced, 12pt font, preferably Times New Roman. Formats can be .doc, .docx, .pdf, or .odt. We actually prefer .odt.

Finally, please tell us when and where the piece was rejected, and if the editor rejecting it gave a reason. Please not that while we'd love to feature everyone's rejected work, releasing things via a podcast is just not possible. We only have limited time to record. We read everything and will work with you to edit it if we want to record it.
